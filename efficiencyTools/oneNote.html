Boundless Paper

A ambiguous feature of OneNote is its boundless paper, which means that when writing in OneNote, there is no bounder, you can always go more and more left or down. Therefore, when I take note in class, I can always keep my writing position in the middle of the iPad screen.

Synchronization

Another reason for me to choose OneNote is for its feature of synchronization. Here is my demand: after taking notes in class, I also need to review these nodes after class and before the exam to assure my comprehension about these notes. Thus, I need an app that allow me to do hand writing and at the same time I can also access to my notes from my computer and my phons. In this perspective, OneNote become my best choice.
